Six - Month Courses

These courses are ideal for those who wish to build a strong foundation for further training. By the end of the program, participants will gain practical skills they can apply in different fields.

First Aid

Purpose: To provide first aid awareness and basic life support

Content:

  • Wounds and bleeding
  • Burns and fractures
  • Emergency Management
  • Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)
  • Respiratory distress

Fee: R1500

Life Skills

Purpose: To provide skills to navigate basic life necessities

Content:

  • Opening a bank account
  • Basic labour law (know your rights)
  • Basic reading and literacy
  • Basic numeric literacy

Fee: R1500

Landscaping

Purpose: To provide landscaping services for new and established gardens

Content:

  • Indigenous and exotic plants and trees
  • Fixed structures (fountains, benches, tables)
  • Aesthetics of plant shapes and colours
  • Garden layout

Fee: R1500

Sewing

Purpose: To provide alterations and new garment tailoring services

Content:

  • Types of stitches
  • Threading a sewing machine
  • Sewing buttons, zips, hems, and seams
  • Alterations
  • Designing and sewing new garments

Fee: R1500
