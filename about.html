Empowering The Nation - Skills for a Brighter Future

Established in 2022, Empowering the Nation is dedicated to providing professional, structured training programmes for domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg. Our six-week short courses and 12-week learnerships are designed to enhance employability, raise earning potential, and equip individuals with practical skills that meet today’s household and business needs.

Our Vision

To create a society where every domestic worker and gardener has the opportunity to grow professionally, achieve financial independence, and contribute to building stronger, more sustainable communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality training programmes that upskill and empower individuals. We support entrepreneurship, strengthen communities through education, and bridge the skills gap between employers and workers.

“Empowerment begins with education. When people have the skills, they have the power to change their future.” – Precious Radebe

Our Impact

Since our founding, Empowering the Nation has been committed to transforming lives through education. Every participant who completes a course contributes to a growing network of skilled, confident individuals driving positive change in their own lives, households, and communities.
